# Week 1 - HRa2 - Research questions and hypotheses


## What you will learn

- To formulate a solid research question based on a management problem.
- To formulate good null- and alternative hypotheses (H<sub>0</sub> and H<sub>a</sub>/H<sub>1</sub>).

## What you need to prepare

- Read these chapters in Doing Research / What is research?
  - 4 - Central question and objective
  - 7 - Quantitative data collection methods
  - 13 - Processing quantitative data: preparation
- Watch the video below.
  - [Slides](https://moodle.zuyd.nl/draftfile.php/738493/user/draft/837095965/Research%20Questions%20and%20Hypotheses.pdf) to the video.
- Choose three out of seven cases ([English](https://moodle.zuyd.nl/draftfile.php/738493/user/draft/837095965/Cases%20-%20formulating%20research%20questions.pdf), [Dutch](https://moodle.zuyd.nl/draftfile.php/738493/user/draft/837095965/Casussen%20-%20opstellen%20onderzoeksvragen.pdf)) to prepare before class.
  - Bring your results to class, and be prepared to explain them.
