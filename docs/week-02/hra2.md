# Week 2 - HRa2

## Meeting 2 (HRA2) - Reliability, Validity and Sampling

### What you will learn

- Know strategies on how to sample participants in an experiment.
- Understand the different methods of sampling.
- Understand the concept of validity.
- Explain the types of validity.
- Understand the concept of reliability.
- Know the difference between validity and reliability.
