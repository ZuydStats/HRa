# Week 2 - HRa1

## Meeting 1 (HRA1) - Quantitative analyses and Type I & II errors

### What you will learn

- Refresh your knowledge on how to formulate hypotheses (H<sub>0</sub> and H<sub>a</sub>/H<sub>1</sub>).
- Define a Type I and a Type II error.
- Understand how a Type I and Type II error is made.
- Evaluate the potential costs of a Type I and Type II error.

### What you will need to prepare

- Watch the video on Type I and II errors.

  - This can be digital or a hand-crafted version.
  - Using this, you should be able to construct a H<sub>0</sub> and H<sub>A</sub>.
  - Include your definition of what Type I and Type II errors are.
