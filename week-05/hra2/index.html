Week 5 - HRa2 - TTest

What you will learn

  • You know how to apply the t-test in Jamovi.
  • You know how to interpret the t-test output in Jamovi.
  • You know what assumptions need to be met.
  • You understand the difference between the paired-samples t-test and the independent t-test.

What you will need to prepare

  • Read the following in the Jamovi book:
    • Chapter 11 - Comparing two means (except §1 and §5)
    • §2 - The one-sample t-test
    • §3 - The independent-samples t-test (Student test)
    • §4 - Completing the test
    • §6 - The paired-samples t-test
    • §7 - One-sided tests
    • §8 - Effect size
    • §9 - Checking the normality of a sample
    • §10 - Testing non-normal data
    • §11 - Summary

Studying? Try a recap

  • After reading the book and watching the video above, execute a t-test on the difference in absenteeism, based on whether employees have a health plan or not. Report on your findings. Then, watch the video below and check whether you did this correctly.
  • Last, execute two t-tests between two relevant variables in the personnel data or the customer data of the Myriad Hotel and be prepared to show and explain this during the meeting.
